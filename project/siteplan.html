Safer Samoa - Cyclone & Emergency Prep

A living site plan for the Week 6 project.

Site Name

Safer Samoa: Cyclone & Emergency Prep

Why: Clear, place-specific, and action-oriented for families preparing for cyclones and other hazards.

Optional domain idea: safersamoa.org (availability not required)

Site Purpose

Provide Samoa-focused preparedness: step-by-step cyclone prep, a Family Kit Builder saved with localStorage, a Contacts & Shelters page, and a Tips & Contact page. The site will be responsive, accessible, and performance-minded (optimized/lazy-loaded images).

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  • "What supplies - and how much water per person should my family store for a cyclone?"
  • "Where is the nearest shelter and who do I call during a storm?"

Color Schema

  • Deep Teal
    - headings, links, key accents.
  • Warm Orange #efac3e - buttons, badges, emphasis.
  • Ink #111827 - body text. Paper #f8fafc - page background.

These colors are applied throughout this document (see headings, tag, text, and background).

Typography

  • Poppins - headings and logo marks.
  • Inter - body text and UI.

Both fonts are loaded in the <head> and in use on this page.

Planned Pages

  1. Home - overview + cyclone prep steps (hero image is lazy-loaded).
  2. Family Kit Builder - interactive checklist (arrays, events, DOM), saved to localStorage with template literals for output.
  3. Contacts & Shelters - emergency numbers and simple image map of shelters.
  4. Tips & Contact - safety tips + accessible HTML form; short quiz using conditionals.
